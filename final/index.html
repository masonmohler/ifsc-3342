About Us

The Lupus Foundation of Arkansas, Inc. is a 501c3 nonprofit organization originally based in Hot Springs using volunteers since October 1993 to bring public awareness for the cause of lupus throughout the state of Arkansas. Headquartered in Little Rock, we serve communities throughout Arkansas through education, support, outreach, and advocacy. We believe that no one should face lupus alone, and we are here to ensure every Arkansan touched by the disease has access to the resources, care, and support they need.

Our Mission

Supporting patients, families, and caregivers through every stage of their journey.

Providing reliable information and resources to those living with the disease.

Collaborating with medical professionals and researchers to improve treatment and outcomes
